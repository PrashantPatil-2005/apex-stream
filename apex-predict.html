Feature Explanations
Qualifying Position

Grid position from Saturday qualifying. Pole position wins ~40% of races historically — the single strongest predictor.

Driver ELO Rating

A dynamic skill rating that adjusts after each race based on performance vs expected finish. Higher ELO = more consistent top finishes.

Constructor Momentum

Rolling average of constructor points in the last 3 races. Captures team form, car development trajectory, and reliability trends.

Circuit Win History

Number of podiums the driver has at this specific circuit. Some drivers consistently excel at specific track layouts.

Gap to Pole (ms)

Millisecond delta between driver's qualifying lap and pole position. Smaller gaps indicate closer competitiveness on race day.

Tyre Strategy Score

A composite metric rating the expected tyre degradation and strategic options available. Accounts for compound choices and stint planning.

Weather Conditions

Encoded weather state: dry, damp, wet. Wet races introduce variance that can override qualifying advantage — rain specialists benefit.

Pit Stop Delta

Team's average pit stop time vs the field average. Faster stops = less time lost per strategy window. Top teams save ~1s per stop.
